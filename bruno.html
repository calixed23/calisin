Bruno Mars

Peter Gene Hernandez, known professionally as Bruno Mars, is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. With his retro-inspired sound and wide range of musical influences, including pop, R&B, funk, and soul, Mars has dominated the charts with hits like "Just the Way You Are" (2010), "Uptown Funk" (2014), and "24K Magic" (2016). He has won numerous Grammy Awards, including Album of the Year, and is known for his energetic live performances and charismatic stage presence.
