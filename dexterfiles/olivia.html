OLIVIA

Olivia Rodrigo is an American singer, songwriter, and actress who rose to fame in 2021 with her debut album SOUR. The album's chart-topping singles like "drivers license," "deja vu," and "good 4 u" made her one of the most prominent voices in pop music. Her raw, emotionally charged lyrics about teenage heartbreak resonated with listeners worldwide, and she won multiple awards, including three Grammy Awards in 2022, cementing her place as a breakout pop star.
