Gary Valenciano

Gary Valenciano is a Filipino singer, dancer, and actor known for his dynamic performances and vocal talent. He is considered one of the greatest performers in the Philippines, with a career that has spanned over three decades. Valenciano's hits include "Take Me Out of the Dark" and "Shout for Joy," and he is known for his Christian-themed music as well as his energetic, theatrical live performances. He also serves as a judge on The Voice Philippines and is an active philanthropist.
