kanye west

Kanye West is an American rapper, producer, and fashion designer known for his boundary-pushing work in both music and fashion. Starting with his critically acclaimed album The College Dropout (2004), West revolutionized hip-hop by blending soulful samples with innovative production. He has released multiple iconic albums, including Late Registration (2005) and My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy (2010). A controversial figure, West has also made headlines for his outspoken nature and ventures into fashion, notably with his Yeezy brand.
