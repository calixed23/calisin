Lea Salonga

Lea Salonga is a Filipino singer and actress, best known for her Tony Award-winning performance in Miss Saigon (1991). She gained international fame as a child prodigy and went on to have a successful career in musical theater, television, and film. Apart from her theater work, she has also been a judge on The Voice Philippines and has released albums in both English and Filipino. Her powerful and versatile voice has earned her a lasting place in the global entertainment industry.
