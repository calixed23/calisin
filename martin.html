Martin Nievera

Martin Nievera is a Filipino singer and television host, often referred to as the "Concert King" of the Philippines. With his powerful tenor voice, Nievera has become one of the most prominent figures in Filipino pop and ballad music, with hits like "Be My Lady" and "You Are My Song." He has had a successful career spanning over three decades, releasing numerous albums, and remains a mainstay in Filipino music and entertainment through his concerts and TV appearances.
