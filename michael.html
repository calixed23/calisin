Michael Pangilinan

Michael Pangilinan is a Filipino singer, best known for his appearances on various TV talent shows, including The Voice Philippines and Your Face Sounds Familiar. He is recognized for his powerful, soulful voice and has made a name for himself with his covers of classic songs and original music. Pangilinan's career in the music industry has been marked by his successful albums and singles, showcasing his versatility in both pop and R&B genres.
